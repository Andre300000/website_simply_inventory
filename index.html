Introduction

Simply Inventory is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Data: Name, email address, organization details when signing in or contacting support.
  • Inventory Data: Items, quantities, categories, and metadata entered by users.
  • Device Permissions: Camera (for barcode scanning), storage (for exporting/importing files).
  • Usage Information: Interaction logs and analytics, collected anonymously via Firebase.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve core app functionality.
  • To personalize the experience and respond to user inquiries.
  • To ensure data synchronization across devices.
  • To analyze trends and app performance (aggregate only).

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Selling: We never sell your data to third parties.
  • Trusted Services: We use Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time via the app.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Revoke permissions (camera/storage) through your device settings.
  • Request full account and data deletion by contacting support.

Children’s Privacy

Simply Inventory is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.
